# Display Troubleshooting Notes

## Topic
CompTIA A+ hardware troubleshooting

## What I learned today
- A dim laptop screen can be caused by a bad inverter or backlight issue.
- If the laptop boots but the screen is very faint, the display is receiving an image but not enough light.
- Troubleshooting means checking the most likely cause first.

## Key terms
- LCD: Laptop display screen
- Inverter: Provides power to the screen backlight on older laptops
- Backlight: The light behind the screen that makes the image visible
- Video card: Sends the image signal to the display

## Example question
A laptop boots normally, but the LCD is extremely faint.

Most likely cause: Inverter or backlight issue.

## My explanation
If you can barely see the image, the computer is still producing video. The problem is not usually the video card. The issue is more likely with the screen lighting system.
